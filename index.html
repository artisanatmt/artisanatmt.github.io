About Us

ARTISANAT M&T London Ltd is a trusted carpentry and construction company based in West London. Founded by Mikhail Mikulyak, we specialize in custom joinery, fire door installation, and complete building solutions with respect for traditional architecture.

We are VAT registered, CSCS certified, and have experience with both private and commercial clients. Our approach combines traditional skills with modern techniques for outstanding results.

Our Services

  • ✅ Fire-rated door installation (EI30 and more)
  • ✅ Engineered flooring and laminate installation
  • ✅ Loft conversions and extensions
  • ✅ Facade cladding, soffits, and gutters
  • ✅ Bespoke joinery and furniture fitting
  • ✅ Garage builds and kitchen fitting
